Teaching

I teach courses on artificial intelligence, natural language processing, digital humanities, and data sciences at the intersection of computation and communication. Prospective graduate students should contact me directly.

Courses

  • AI in Action: Build and Apply
    Hands-on introduction to AI applications across industries, with case studies in media, journalism, cybersecurity, marketing, finance, and healthcare.
  • GEN/AI and NLP for Media Innovation
    Generative AI and NLP applied to media innovation, with emphasis on Arabic content and responsible deployment.
  • Intro to Social Computing
    Computational approaches for studying social behavior, online communities, and digital platforms.
  • Data Sciences and Text Analytics
    Data science fundamentals and text analytics for communication research, including Arabic text processing.
  • Methods in Digital Humanities
    Research methods and applied projects in digital humanities, covering text analysis, corpus-based approaches, and digital curation.
  • Coding for Humanities
    Programming fundamentals applied to humanities research, including Python, data wrangling, and reproducible analysis.
  • Digital Communication and Media
    Digital communication theory and methods with applications to Arabic and global media.
  • Emerging Technologies and Applications
    Survey of emerging digital technologies, AI systems, and their applications to research and practice.
  • Doctoral Pro Seminar in Digital Humanities
    Doctoral seminar on digital humanities scholarship, research design, and methodological foundations.
